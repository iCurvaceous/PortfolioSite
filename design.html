DESIGN

I'm not the average pretty young lady in her early 20's. I was born to play games, literally. My gamer parents introduced console games, hand held games and even computer games when I was really young. Anime shows such as DBZ, Yugioh, Pokemon and Sailormoon was the norm. I thought school was too easy back then that I was self-taught in Photoshop since I was 9 years old.( If you would like to look at the art I draw in my free time, then click this link to visit my DeviantArt Page). I was ranking top 15 in my territory in grades. I realized I had a talent and started drawing at the age of 11. I graduated high school at 16 and then I experienced tough financial situations with my family and I also realized I didn't know what I wanted to actually do. Everything was a hobby for me and I could not have seen any of it as a job career back then. I went to college in general studies to try to figure out what I wanted to do. I tried learning psychology but it wasn't what I really wanted despite my love to help others.
